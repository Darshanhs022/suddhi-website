From Milk to Ghee

Each jar of SUDDHI ghee carries a story of patience and tradition — a quiet ritual practiced with the same care every single time.

01

Fresh milk is gently boiled and cooled to the right temperature.

02

It is then cultured overnight to set slowly into curd.

03

The curd is hand-churned to carefully separate pure butter.

04

The butter is gently heated until it turns into golden, aromatic ghee.

Why Ghee Matters

Moderate consumption of ghee support digestion and gut health. It provides sustained energy and enhances the flavor and satiety of meals.

Ghee naturally contains fat-soluble vitamins such as Vitamin A, D, E and K, which help in nutrient absorption when consumed as part of a balanced diet.
